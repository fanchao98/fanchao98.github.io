Biography

Fanchao Zhong is currently a Ph.D candidate at the Interdisciplinary Research Center of Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Haisen Zhao and Prof. Lin Lu. Before that, he received Bachelor’s degree in 2021 from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Lin Lu.

His research interest lie in computer graphics, focusing on geometric computation and its application for digital fabrication, including additive manufacturing and subtractive manufacturing.
